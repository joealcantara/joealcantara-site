## Working Papers

### Epistemic vs Instrumental AI: A Conceptual Foundation
**Status:** In development
**Target:** IACAP 2027 Conference
**Expected:** 2027

Exploring the conceptual viability of separating epistemic AI (truth-tracking systems) from instrumental AI (goal-pursuing systems). Developing philosophical definitions and examining safety implications of prioritizing epistemic over instrumental functions in AI design.
